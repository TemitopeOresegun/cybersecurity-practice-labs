# What is Networking Notes

## Overview
Networking allows devices to communicate and share resources. Cybersecurity depends on understanding how networks operate.

## Key Concepts
- **Types of Networks**:
  - LAN (Local Area Network)
  - WAN (Wide Area Network)
  - Wireless Networks
- **Protocols**: Rules that devices follow to communicate (TCP/IP, HTTP, DHCP)
- **IP Addressing**: Logical address to identify devices
- **MAC Addressing**: Physical address for device identification on LAN

## Takeaways
- Networking fundamentals are essential for cybersecurity
- Understanding protocols helps analyze traffic and detect attacks
- Lab practice improves conceptual understanding
